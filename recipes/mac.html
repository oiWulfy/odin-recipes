Cauliflower Mac & Cheese

Description

A special Korean inspired Gourmet Vegan Burger. Crispy portobello mushroom with a sticky apple & soy glaze.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs Olive Oils
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, mincedl
  • 4 Spring Onions, finely chopped
  • 1 Small Head Cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • Handful Sun-dried Tomatoes, chopped small
  • 2 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 tsp Sweet Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbs Whole Grain Mustard
  • 3 tbs Plain or Gluten Free Flour
  • 3 cups/750ml Non Dairy Milk
  • 2 tbs of Nutritional Yeast
  • 3/4 cup/75g Vegan Cheese, grated
  • 1 can Chickpeas, drained
  • Juice Half a Lemon
  • Salt & pepper to season
  • 2 cups/250g of Cooked Macaroni pasta or Pasta of your choice

    • Topping

  • 1 cup/100g Breadcrumbs
  • 2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme

Steps

  1. Pre heat your oven to 180 degrees C.
  2. Place a large saucepan over a low heat and add a little oil followed by the garlic, spring onions, cauliflower & sun-dried tomatoes. Saute the mixture for 3-4 minutes until everything has soften slightly. Next add the thyme, paprika & mustard & cook for another minute before adding the flour. Continuously stir the mixture when youâ€™ve added the flour so that it does not burn. After a couple of minutes, stir in the non dairy milk, nutritional yeast & vegan cheese.
  3. Let the liquid bubble away for 4-5 minutes or until it is thick & creamy. Stir in the chickpeas, lemon juice and cooked pasta.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish & sprinkle over breadcrumbs & fresh thyme leaves.
  5. Place the dish into the oven for 10-15 minutes or until the topping has gone crisp & golden.
  6. Serve with salad or greens.
